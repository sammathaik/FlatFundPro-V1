## ‚ö° Quick Troubleshooting

**No notifications showing?**
- Check if payments have been submitted
- Verify occupant_type is Owner or Tenant
- Ensure phone numbers are valid

**Can't see all notifications?**
- You may be an apartment admin (limited view)
- Super admins see all notifications

**Filters not working?**
- Try "Clear All Filters"
- Refresh page (navigate away and back)
