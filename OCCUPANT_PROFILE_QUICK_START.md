# Occupant Profile & Pending Payments - Quick Start Guide

## How to Use the New Features

### 1. Accessing Your Profile

**Login:**
```
1. Click "Login" on the landing page
2. Mobile login is the default (or switch to Email)
3. For mobile: Enter your registered mobile number
4. Verify with OTP
```

**Navigate to Profile:**
```
1. After login, you'll land on the Dashboard
2. Click the "Profile" tab in the header
3. You'll see your profile information and pending payments
```

## 2. Editing Your Profile

### Editable Fields

**Name:**
- Click "Edit Profile" button
- Update your name in the Name field
- Click "Save Changes"

**Mobile Number:**
- Click "Edit Profile" button
- Enter your 10-digit mobile number
- System will validate and normalize it
- Click "Save Changes"

**Email Address:**
- Click "Edit Profile" button
- Update your email address
- System will validate format
- Click "Save Changes"

### Read-Only Fields

The following fields **cannot** be changed by you:
- **Occupant Type** (Owner/Tenant)
- **Apartment & Block**
- **Flat Number**

**Why?** These are managed by your committee for governance and accuracy.

**Need to change?** Contact your committee members.

## 4. Making a Payment (One-Click)

### Step-by-Step:

**Step 1: Click "Pay Now"**
- Find the payment you want to make
- Click the blue/red "Pay Now" button
- A payment modal will open

**Step 2: Review Pre-filled Information**
- Collection details are already filled
- Balance amount is pre-populated
- Payment date defaults to today

**Step 3: Fill Payment Details**

Required fields:
- ‚úÖ **Payment Amount**: Edit if paying partial amount
- ‚úÖ **Payment Date**: Select the date you made the payment
- ‚úÖ **Payment Mode**: Choose from dropdown (UPI/Net Banking/Cards/Cash/Cheque)
- ‚úÖ **Screenshot**: Upload your payment proof (max 5MB)

Optional fields:
- Transaction Reference Number
- WhatsApp Reminder opt-in

**Step 4: Upload Screenshot**
- Click the upload area
- Select image from your device
- Preview will appear
- Click again to change image

**Step 5: WhatsApp Reminders (Optional)**
- Check the box if you want WhatsApp reminders
- You'll receive notifications 7, 3, and 1 day before due dates
- Only works if you've provided mobile number

**Step 6: Submit**
- Click "Submit Payment" button
- Wait for success message
- Modal will close automatically
- Dashboard refreshes with updated data

### What Happens After Submission?

1. ‚úÖ Payment submitted with status "Received"
2. üìß You receive an email acknowledgment
3. üë• Committee gets notified for review

## 5. Understanding Payment Status

### In Pending Payments:

- **Due**: Payment not yet submitted
- **Overdue**: Past due date, may have late fees
- **Partially Paid**: Some amount submitted, balance remains

### In Transaction History (Dashboard tab):

- **Received**: Submitted, awaiting committee review
- **Reviewed**: Committee has reviewed it
- **Approved**: Payment approved and recorded

## 8. Common Questions

**Q: Can I pay partial amounts?**
A: Yes! Edit the payment amount field to any value you want to pay.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: Contact your committee immediately. They can reject the payment and you can resubmit.

**Q: Why can't I change my flat number?**
A: This is managed by the committee to ensure data accuracy. Contact them for changes.

**Q: Do I need to enable WhatsApp notifications?**
A: No, it's optional. But it helps you stay on top of due dates.

**Q: What image formats are supported?**
A: JPG, PNG, and most image formats. Max size is 5MB.

**Q: Can I submit multiple payments at once?**
A: Currently, one at a time. Click "Pay Now" on each pending payment separately.

**Q: Will my payment be approved immediately?**
A: No, the committee will review and approve within their timeline.

## 10. Troubleshooting

**Issue: Can't upload screenshot**
- Check file size (must be under 5MB)
- Try compressing the image
- Ensure it's a valid image format

**Issue: Mobile number not saving**
- Ensure it's exactly 10 digits
- Remove any spaces or special characters
- Check error message for details

**Issue: Email not saving**
- Ensure format is correct (e.g., name@example.com)
- No spaces allowed
- Check for typos

**Issue: Pending payments not loading**
- Refresh the page
- Check your internet connection
- Click "Try again" if error appears

**Issue: Payment modal not opening**
- Refresh the page
- Try clicking "Pay Now" again
- Clear browser cache if persistent
